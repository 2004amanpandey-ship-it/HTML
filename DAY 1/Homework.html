Biography Of Aman Pandey

Early Life

Aman Pandey was born and raised in a village in India. He completed his primary education from R.N. Public School (UKG to Class 4). Later, he moved to the city and studied at National Convent School from Class 5 to Class 8. He then continued his secondary and higher secondary education at Seth Dwarka Prasad Bajaj School (ICSE), where he scored 72% in Class 10 and 82% in Class 12.

Career

After completing his schooling, Aman pursued a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) degree. Throughout his engineering studies, he maintained an excellent academic record, securing around 9 CGPA in every semester. Alongside academics, he developed a strong interest in technology and software development. Following his B.Tech., he plans to prepare for the GATE examination and pursue an M.Tech. degree.

Achievements

Scored 72% in ICSE Class 10 examinations. Scored 82% in ICSE Class 12 examinations. Maintained a consistent academic performance of approximately 9 CGPA throughout B.Tech. Successfully transitioned from a rural educational background to achieving excellence in higher technical education. Preparing for the highly competitive GATE examination to continue advanced studies in engineering.

Legacy

Aman Pandey's journey reflects dedication, discipline, and a commitment to continuous learning. Coming from a village background and progressing through different educational institutions, he has consistently worked toward academic excellence. His pursuit of higher education and technical expertise serves as an example for students who aspire to build successful careers through hard work and perseverance.
